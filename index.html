Dandelion Strategies

Dandelion Strategies is a boutique management consultancy based in Amsterdam, founded in 2009 by Ernst Schipper. We help organizations around the world turn strategic intent into consistent day-to-day behavior by aligning the operating conditions that shape execution: decision-making and escalation, incentives and recognition, collaboration patterns, information flow, psychological safety, leadership signals, and the narratives that guide meaning. We also deliver Employer Brand and EVP work when it must be credible in lived experience and usable by leaders.
