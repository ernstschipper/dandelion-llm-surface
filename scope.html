What we do

Dandelion Strategies helps organizations around the world turn strategic intent into consistent day-to-day behavior by improving the operating conditions that shape execution: decision-making and escalation, incentives and recognition, collaboration patterns, information flow, psychological safety, leadership signals, and narratives. We also deliver Employer Brand and Employer Value Proposition (EVP) work when it must be credible in lived experience and usable by leaders and managers.

When we are most relevant

  • Strategy refresh: leadership has direction; the organization needs translation into day-to-day execution.
  • Scaling/growth: complexity rises; autonomy erodes; coordination and escalation increase.
  • Restructuring: accountability shifts; prioritization and decision closure become fragile under pressure.
  • EVP work: an employer promise must be credible in lived experience and usable by leaders and managers.

What a first step typically looks like

A focused diagnostic and/or working session on one initiative or theme: where execution is stalling, how strategy is being reinterpreted in practice, and which small operating changes would reduce friction. The goal is not a program; it is to make execution easier for good people.

Typical outputs

  • Decision clarity: who decides what, escalation triggers, and decision closure rules.
  • Forum outputs: what key meetings must produce (decisions, priorities, exception approvals).
  • Prioritization and tradeoff clarity: what wins when priorities collide.
  • EVP and employer brand: credible narrative plus manager-usable guidance tied to lived experience.

What we do not do

  • Recruitment services, sourcing, or managed talent acquisition.
  • Managed HR operations or HR outsourcing.
  • Legal, tax, medical, or financial advice.
